This assignment introduces you to the art of digital compositing to challenge expectations of truth and reality in photography. In our digitally saturated culture, we come into contact with images on a daily basis and often accept them as truth. “Deep fakes” can often make us question what is real and what is not.

What is this assignment about?

How did I approach this assignment?

Peter Pan appealed to me because he lived in a magical world and refused to grow up.

I dreamed of living in an extraordinary world. Reflecting on my childhood, I realize Peter Pan's character has profoundly impacted my personality. I still hold on to that childlike sense of wonder as an adult.

This assignment required me to added the characters like animals, the galaxy, the ocean, mountains, the moon, etc.

My Reflection

This assignment allowed me to relive those childhood dreams and took me back to the world of Neverland. It reminded me that whenever I take a break from the stresses of everyday life and immerse myself in a good book or movie, I will return to reality feeling refreshed and re-energized.
